What To Do When Your Flight Is Disrupted

1. Know Your Rights (EU261)

If your flight departs from the EU or is with an EU airline, you are entitled to up to €600 compensation for delays over 3 hours. Do not accept vouchers if you prefer cash.

2. Secure Proof

Take photos of the departures board. Save your boarding pass. Ask staff for a written reason for the delay. This evidence is crucial for your claim.

3. The Right to Care

For significant delays, the airline MUST provide food, drinks, and potentially hotel accommodation. Keep receipts for everything if they don't provide vouchers.

4. The Premium Alternative

Stuck in a crowded terminal? Private aviation (Empty Legs) can often get you to your destination faster than rebooking commercial, especially during strikes.
