Know your rights
Regulation EU 261/2004

Most airlines bank on you not knowing the law. Under European Regulation 261/2004, passengers are entitled to financial compensation for flights departing from EU airports or arriving in the EU on an EU carrier.

  • ✓

    Delay over 3 hours

    You are entitled to compensation if you arrive at your destination more than 3 hours late.

  • ✓

    Cancellation

    If informed less than 14 days before departure, you may be eligible for up to €600.
